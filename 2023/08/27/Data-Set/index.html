Low Altitude Disaster Imagery (LADI) Dataset

The Low Altitude Disaster Imagery (LADI) Dataset provides airborne image data for states along the Atlantic and Gulf Coast, providing data support for disaster control from 2015 to 2019.

URL：https://registry.opendata.aws/ladi/

Potential applications：Hurricane prediction、search and rescue support and disaster response

Brief Introduction: The images in this dataset have low altitude, oblique perspective, and disaster related features, providing relatively novel data for computer vision training (there are few datasets with these features). This dataset is divided into five categories to record the Atlantic coast and Gulf Coast during the Atlantic hurricane and spring flood seasons since 2015.

By analyzing the image features during the occurrence of flood disasters, it is possible to predict such natural disaster scenes and provide reference for disaster control.
